Biography:

Alyssa Miller was born on July 4, 1989 in Los Angeles, California, USA. She began her modeling career when her father sent test photos of her to a Los Angeles office of IMG Models.

In 2006, she appeared on the cover of Vogue, establishing her career as a model. She also graced the cover of Italian version of Elle in July 2010 and October 2012.

She then went on to make her Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue debut in 2011, which were photographed in Fiji and Hawaii. She recently appeared as the mermaid in the music video for “Mermaid” by Train.
