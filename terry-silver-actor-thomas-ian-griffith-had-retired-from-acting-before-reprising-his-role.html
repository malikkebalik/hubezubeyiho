Terry Silver Actor Thomas Ian Griffith Had Retired From Acting Before Reprising His Role

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Celebrity / TV

Thomas Ian Griffith tells the Cobra Kai Kompanion podcast what brought him back to the role of Terry Silver. 'The Karate Kid Part III' launched Griffith's acting career, but since 2007 he's had a different job in Hollywood. That is, until 'Cobra Kai' gave him the chance to play Terry Silver again.

Published on August 11, 2021

2 min read

Thomas Ian Griffith will return as Terry Silver on Cobra Kai Season 4. Not only will it be his first time playing the character since 1989’s The Karate Kid Part III, but it will be his first acting role since 2007. Griffith hadn’t retired completely from Hollywood. Here’s what he’s been doing instead. 

Griffith was a guest on the Cobra Kai Kompanion podcast on July 30. Previewing his role in Season 4 of Cobra Kai, Griffith explained why he came out of acting retirement. Cobra Kai Season 4 premieres on Netflix this December. 

Between ‘Karate Kid III’ and ‘Cobra Kai,’ Thomas Ian Griffith became a screenwriter

After The Karate Kid Part III, Griffith began writing. He wrote scripts for some of his own movie vehicles, and others in which he did not star. Griffith was a writer on the TV series Grimm. His wife is actor Mary Page Keller who also divides her time between acting and writing.

“The irony that I go back to, I keep saying to Mary, she’s so picky but she’s done a few things,” Griffith said. “She did a film Beginners that she’s so wonderful in. I’m like, ‘You gotta keep acting.’ She was like, ‘You know what? I enjoy the writing too.’ So she’s gone back and forth a little more than I have but she always says, ‘You’re going to go back, you’re going to go back. It’s like, ‘Nah, I’m happy with what I’m doing.’”

‘Cobra Kai’ Season 4 was an offer Thomas Ian Griffith couldn’t refuse

Griffith was planning to continue his writing career. That’s when Cobra Kai creators Josh Heald, Jon Hurwitz and Hayden Schlossberg told him they wanted Terry Silver back in season 4.

“Then this came along when the guys called about Cobra Kai saying they’re bringing back Terry Silver,” Griffith said. “At first, it was like what’s Terry Silver been up to for the last 30 years? Then when they mapped out their plan, oh my God, I cannot say no to that. That sounds incredible. So I jumped on. That’s what I love about those guys. When they decide they’re going to do something, they do it and they do it so well.”

Thomas Ian Griffith had to fit the show in between writing assignments 

Since Grimm ended, Griffith has continued writing. He and Kellar wrote Dolly Parton’s Heartstrings and they were busy up until Cobra Kai Season 4 needed him.

“There was rumors, I had a conversation but then I think dates got pushed,” Griffith said. “Mary and I had a deal at Warner Bros. We were writing something for them. The timing just worked out. It happened so fast. It was like in December and they said, ‘You’re going to be in Atlanta working mid January,’ whatever it was. So it didn’t give me too much time to think about it.”
