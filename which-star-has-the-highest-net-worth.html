Which Star Has the Highest Net Worth?

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Legally Blonde first hit movie theatres back in 2001. Reese Witherspoon says it’s the one movie her fans ask about most. Which cast member is the richest? Here’s a rundown of each actor’s net worth.

Matthew Davis

Net worth: $2 million

Matthew Davis played Elle’s boyfriend, Warner. One of his early roles was in the 2000 movie Tigerland. The following year he played Joe in Pearl Harbor. In 2006, Davis joined the cast of What About Brian, in which he played Adam Hillman for 24 episodes until 2007. In 2009, he joined The Vampire Diaries cast, playing Alaric Saltzman for 123 episodes until 2017. Some of Davis’ other roles include appearances in Legacies, Cult, Damages, and Shadow of Fear. As of this writing, Davis has an estimated net worth of $2 million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Victor Garber

Net worth: $5 million

Victor Garber played Professor Callahan. One of his early roles was in the 1973 production Godspell. He played the role of Jesus. In 1985, he appeared in the movie Private Sessions. His other roles include appearances in Sleepless in Seattle, The First Wives Club, Titanic, Argo, and Alias. As of this writing, Garber has an estimated net worth of $5 million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Selma Blair

Net worth: $6 million

Selma Blair plays Vivian in Legally Blonde. One of her early roles was in a 1995 episode of The Adventures of Pete & Pete titled “Das Bus.” The following year, she appeared on The Dana Carvey Show. Blair made her film debut in the 1996 movie The Broccoli Theory. She rose to fame after starring in the television series Zoe, Duncan, Jack & Jane. Blair played Zoe for 26 episodes from 1999 to 2000. As of this writing, Blair has an estimated net worth of $6 million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Jennifer Coolidge

Net worth: $8 million

Jennifer Coolidge, known for her performance in the “bend and snap,” plays Paulette Parcelle. One of her early roles is in a 1993 episode of Seinfeld titled “The Masseuse,” in which she played the character Jodi. Coolidge made her film debut in the 1995 movie Not of This Earth. A few years later she played Stifler’s mom in the 1999 movie American Pie. In 2004, Coolidge Bobbie Morgenstern in the sitcom Joey.  She remained part of the cast until the series ended in 2006. Some of her other roles include appearances in The White Lotus, The Fungies, 2 Broke Girls, and The Secret Life of the American Teenager.

Ali Larter

Net worth: $10 million

Ali Larter plays Brooke Taylor Windham. One of her early roles was in a 1997 episode of the television series Suddenly Susan. The following year, she appeared in an episode of Chicago Hope. In 2006, Larter joined the cast of Heroes, in which she played Niki Sanders, Tracy Strauss, and Jessica Sanders. Some of her other acting roles include appearances in The Rookie, Pitch, and Legends. As of this writing, Larter has an estimated net worth of $10 million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Luke Wilson

Net worth: $50 million

Luke Wilson plays Emmett in Legally Blonde. One of his early acting roles was in the 1993 short film Bottle Rocket. The following year, Wilson appeared in the movie Telling Lies in America. Wilson’s popularity rose after he starred in Scream 2 as ‘Stab’ Billy.

In 2002, he landed a recurring role on That ‘70s Show. Wilson played Casey Kelso for six episodes until 2005. Wilson’s other acting roles include appearances in Charlie’s Angels, Roadies, The Ridiculous 6, My Super Ex-Girlfriend, and Stargirl. As of this writing, Wilson has an estimated net worth of $50 million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Reese Witherspoon

Net worth: $200 million

Reese Witherspoon plays lead character Elle Woods. She made her film acting debut in the 1991 movie The Man in the Moon. She played the role of Dani Trant. After that, she played Ellie Perkins in the TV movie Wildflower. In 1993, Witherspoon had a recurring role in the television mini-series Return to Lonesome Dove. She played the role of Ferris Dunnigan for four episodes.

Witherspoon began her rise to fame after appearing in the 1996 film Fear, alongside actor Mark Wahlberg. Her recent work includes appearances in The Morning Show, Little Fires Everywhere, Truth Be Told, and Big Little Lies. As of this writing, Witherspoon has an estimated net worth of $50 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
