Vittoria is a Supermodel

On June 7, 1998, Vittoria Ceretti (age 25) was born in Lombardy, Italy. Her father, Giuseppe Ceretti, is the proprietor of a flooring company, while her mother, Francesca Lazzari, manages their household.

According to credible sources, Vittoria embarked on her journey into modeling at the tender age of 14 when she participated in the esteemed Elite Model Look competition in Italy.

She demonstrated remarkable potential and was selected as one of the competition’s finalists, marking the beginning of her illustrious modeling career. Ceretti made her impressive runway debut in the iconic fashion hub of Milan, showcasing the creations of designer Kristina Ti.

Since that pivotal moment, her career has soared to remarkable heights, gracing the catwalk for prestigious fashion houses and renowned stylists such as Salvatore Ferragamo, Dolce & Gabbana, Paco Rabanne, Versace, Burberry, and Louis Vuitton.

Most Searched Model on Vogue

In addition to her successful runway career, Ceretti has established herself as a sought-after figure in fashion editorial.

She graced the covers of renowned magazines such as Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, IO Donna, Grazia, and Glamour, showcasing her versatility and appeal to a broad audience.

Notably, in 2017, Ceretti was among the select seven models featured in Vogue’s special 125th-anniversary edition, further solidifying her status as a prominent figure in the fashion industry.

Just a year later, she achieved the distinction of being the most searched-for model on Vogue’s official website, a testament to her growing popularity and influence.

Currently, Ceretti is under the representation of top-tier model management agencies, including Elite Model Management, The Society Management, MP Stockholm, and Model Management, aligning herself with some of the most prestigious names in the modeling world.
