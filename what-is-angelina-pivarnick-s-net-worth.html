What Is Angelina Pivarnick's Net Worth?

September 2024 · 3 minute read

The cast of Jersey Shore Family Vacation is in a league of its own. And fans have been following their lives ever since The Jersey Shore premiered in 2009. 

Original cast member Angelina Pivarnick has been on and off the show for years, yet she hasn’t seen as much celebrity success as her more popular roommates. So what is Angelina’s net worth, and how does she make her money?

How did Angelina Pivarnick become famous?

The Jersey Shore premiered on MTV over a decade ago, and at the time, no one could predict how successful the show would become. With outrageous personalities like Mike “The Situation” Sorrentino, DJ Pauly DelVecchio, and Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi in the mix, the series became an instant hit.

Alongside roommates Jenni “JWoww” Farley, Sammi Giancola, Ronnie Ortiz-Magro, and Vinny Guadagnino, Angelina Pivarnick became one of the most popular reality stars on TV at the time. However, her tumultuous relationship with certain cast members, and her unwillingness to work, led to her early removal from series. 

Just three episodes into season 1 of The Jersey Shore, Pivarnick was evicted for refusing to work at the local t-shirt shop. She returned for season 2 but left the show again after getting into altercations with Farley, Polizzi, and Sorrentino.

Ultimately, she was replaced by Deena Cortese. But in seasons 5 and 6, Pivarnick briefly visits the roommates to reconnect.

Prior to her return to ‘Jersey Shore Family Vacation’

After leaving The Jersey Shore, Pivarnick appeared on a few different reality series. She was featured on episodes of Couples Therapy, Excused, and How Far Is Tattoo Far?.  In 2011, she was in a wrestling match for Total Nonstop Action Wrestling. And later that year, she made a cameo in Dacav5’s “Dirty Style” music video. 

Around the same time, Pivarnick also released two singles —  “Gotta Go Out” (under the alias Miss AP) and “I’m Hot.” In 2013, in an effort to clear up her stance on gay marriage, the reality star released a single called “Serendipity” with Adam Barta.

In 2017, Pivarnick briefly worked as an EMT for the New York City Fire Department. As reported by Fox Business News, she filed a sexual harassment lawsuit against the FDNY in 2019. The suit claims “she was subjected to a sexually hostile work environment by two of her supervisors, both of whom are lieutenants within the New York City Fire Department’s Bureau of Emergency Medical Services.”

In 2018, Pivarnick rejoined her roommates for season 1 of Jersey Shore Family Vacation. And she’s been a permanent cast member on the show ever since. 

What is ‘Jersey Shore Family Vacation’ star Angelina Pivarnick’s net worth?

In the upcoming episodes, viewers will see Pivarnick marry fiancé Chris Larangeira. And after getting a sneak peek at all the glitz and glam, viewers might be wondering how she could afford such a lavish wedding ceremony. 

Well, according to Cosmopolitan, Angelina Pivarnick is worth $2 million. It is far less than some of her roommates, but is still pretty good considering her roller-coaster relationship with the franchise. However, she recently launched a beauty line called Lashelina. If it does well, there’s a good chance her net worth will rise.
