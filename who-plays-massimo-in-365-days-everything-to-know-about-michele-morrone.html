Who Plays Massimo in '365 Days'? Everything to Know About Michele Morrone

October 2024 · 3 minute read

The recent addition to Netflix, 365 Days, has managed to capture the public’s imagination in a way that no other movie in recent memory has. With a riveting storyline, controversial subjects, and stunning scenery, the movie has quickly become the subject of numerous internet debates and speculation.

One of the film’s stars, Michele Morrone, has also received a great deal of attention from his recent Netflix film, with fans going crazy over his good looks and intense acting. Morrone is no stranger to acting, but his role as Massimo promises to catapult him to the next level of fortune and fame. 

What is ‘365 Days’ about?

Originally released in Poland earlier this spring, 365 Days was released to Netflix in June. Based on a bestselling trilogy of novels, 365 Days combines elements from many genres and has been widely compared to Fifty Shades of Grey due to the themes of domination and subjugation.

The film focuses on a young woman from Warsaw named Laura, and her intense relationship with a wealthy, mysterious man named Massimo.

Massimo becomes intrigued by Laura and kidnaps her, spiriting her away to a brand-new life. He gives Laura 365 days to fall in love with him, promising to release her if she doesn’t. Over the course of the following weeks, the two begin to develop a deep understanding of each other and eventually fall in love.

Still, Laura and Massimo’s future remains uncertain by the end of the film, as tragedy threatens their plans. Viewers are left wondering what will happen next for the pair — and their questions will likely not be answered until the sequel film is eventually released. 

Who is Michele Morrone?

With a roster of top international stars, 365 Days features a truly impressive cast. One of the film’s breakout stars is Morrone, an Italian actor with experience in stage productions as well as television and films. Morrone was born in Italy in 1990 and studied theatre as a young man.

All throughout the early 2000s, Morrone continued to work on stage, gaining experience in everything from drama to contemporary plays. 

Morrone also worked as a model during the early years of his career and has appeared on reality television in Italy. The multi-talented star is a musician as well and has released a solo album, which features his guitar playing.

The film 365 Days includes several songs by Morrone in the soundtrack. Morrone has a YouTube channel that has become immensely popular in recent days, with his videos getting a lot of views.

With all of his credentials, it wasn’t until early 2020 when Morrone really made a splash, however, when he appeared in the role of Massimo Torricelli in 365 Days. 

Will Michele Morrone appear in the sequel to ‘365 Days’?

365 Days became a smash hit right out of the gate, even though many of the film’s more controversial scenes have caused some bad press. Fans are awash with excitement over when they might expect to see the next film in the series — after all, 365 Days is based on a trilogy of books.

While the film’s stars, including Michele Morrone, have confirmed that there are plans in place for a sequel, filming will likely not start until 2021, due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.

Still, the good news for fans is that Morrone is definitely onboard to reprise his role as Massimo. The Italian star will likely be pursuing many other opportunities in the days ahead as well, considering how his newfound popularity is sweeping the globe. Fans can look forward to seeing Morrone’s face in many other projects as the years go on.
