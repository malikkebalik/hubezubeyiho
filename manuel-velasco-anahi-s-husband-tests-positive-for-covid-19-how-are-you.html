Manuel Velasco, Anahis Husband, Tests Positive For COVID-19: How are you?

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Manuel Velasco Coello, Anah í’s husband, tested positive for Coronavirus. So far, it is unknown if the Mexican star is also infected with the virus.

According to Milenio, Velasco Coello is confined in his residence in Mexico and has presented symptoms such as headache and body pain, fever, and respiratory distress, for which he has required strict medical surveillance for his speedy recovery.

At present, the Mexican politician works as Coordinator of the Green Ecologist Party of Mexico (PVEM) in the Senate of Mexico, so he will continue to fulfill his professional commitments remotely to avoid any type of contagion among his colleagues in public functions. , as reported by Milenio.

LatinUS announced that the contagion of Manuel Velasco Coello joins the 27 Mexican senators who in recent months have reported positive cases of the virus.

For her part, Anahi has remained on the sidelines of pronouncing through her social networks about her husband’s diagnosis with COVID-19. However, Milenio stressed that the politician urged the Mexican population to redouble their care in the face of the pandemic that affects the Aztec country.

In mid-December 2020, Velasco Coello was accompanying Anahí during her presentation at the show “Ser o Parecer” by the Mexican group RBD. At that time, the people involved in the production of the show had to be constantly subjected to diagnostic tests for COVID-19.

Manuel Velasco Coello and Anahí: When did they get married?

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Manuel Velasco Coello (@velascom_)

In 2012, Manuel Velasco Coello and Anahi met thanks to a mutual friend. From that moment, the couple was overwhelmed with love and they had a consolidated courtship until April 2015, when they decided to join their lives in marriage in a romantic and intimate ceremony in Mexico.

The couple’s wedding took place on April 25, 2015 at the San Cristobal de las Casas Cathedral in the state of Chiapas in Mexico. For the celebration, the Mexican politician and the singer had the presence of their family and closest friends.

For the link, Anahi wore a wedding dress that was made by the famous designer Benito Santos, while her bridesmaids participated as seamstresses in the elaboration process.

None of the former RBD members attended the couple’s wedding. What is a fact is that they extended their messages of congratulations to Anahí through their digital platforms.

Manuel Velasco Coello and Anahi: How many children do you have?

Manuel Velasco Coello and Anahi are the parents of two children in common: Manuel Velasco Puente, who was born on January 17, 2017, and Emiliano Velasco Puente, born on February 2, 2020.

Since the arrival of her children, Anahí has ​​stayed away from the entertainment industry, so she has focused entirely on her maternal side.

Many fans of the Mexican singer and actress were pleasantly surprised by her appearance on the RBD show “Ser o Parecer” that was held on December 26, 2020. The musical show that was performed virtually was the first appearance of Anahí in an artistic project for several years.

Through her social networks, the interpreter is very close with her followers and has shared very important moments of her children’s growth stage.
